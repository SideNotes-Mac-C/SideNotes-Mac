## 🖌 Overview  

SideNotes mac Professional Suite is a contextual notes environment designed around speed and accessibility, making it practical to capture and reference information throughout the day without the overhead of a full note-taking application.

Panel customization covers width, appearance, and animation style, letting you tune the interaction to feel natural within your specific workflow rather than adapting your behavior to the tool defaults.

Drag-and-drop content import from other applications brings text, images, and files into notes quickly, and drag-out export moves note content to other applications without copy-paste steps.

iCloud sync keeps your notes consistent across all your devices, ensuring that something captured quickly in the side panel is accessible anywhere you need it without any manual sync step.

SideNotes mac Professional Suite is the ambient note layer for users whose work involves constant information processing and who need a capture tool that is always one gesture away, always organized, and always ready.
